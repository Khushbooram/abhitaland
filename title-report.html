Title Report/Title Search

A search report traces the history of a property as to who was the original owner of the property and how it has moved hands over a period of time before reaching the present seller. This is an important part of a housing loan process. A search report is usually prepared by an advocate, who after visiting the registrar’s office and inspecting the property documents, issues a title certificate. A search report states whether the property is unencumbered and has a clear marketable title
